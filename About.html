aphorism 

Giving high value to certain phrases can be extremely beneficial to one's life. To a certain extent, one can even say that these phrases have become a modern life philosophy to many. I can comfortably admit that "fake it until you make it" used to be a core philosophy of mine. However, I have proudly replaced this with "Stay positive, work hard, make it happen".

integration

For all of my projects, my main goal is, and always has been, integration. The perfectly balanced integration, in my opinion, would be the combination of teaching and technology. These two factors are key to a successful outcome.

background

I've worked as an English teacher since 2013. Let's face it. Learning is boring. Wether you're sitting in a class listening to a teacher talk for hours or nose deep in a course book at home, it is boring. However, by integrating technology into the classroom, I've succeeded in keeping my students engagement to their lessons both in class and during study sessions at their homes. Doing this opened up many doors for me.

social

The first projects I had started were activities that took students outside the classroom to practice their English. These projects allowed the Teacher-Student barrier to be a bit more transparent and provide the student with a more relaxed and comfortable learning atmosphere. Besides, who wants to be locked in a room for four hours and listen to a lecture?
