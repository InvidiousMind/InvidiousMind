creativity

Alican Garip

The child in you keeps your heart beating, make sure you give him plenty of food, a lot of space to run around in and be creative.

I believe that creativity is not only a talent, but also a part of one's soul. We can not speak when we are born, therefore, we quickly learn to communicate by other means. I believe that our creative mind is formed at this stage in life, luckily, it's end is at the same stage of many things we live through; death. As long as one breathes, there will be a child inside them. That child is the one that beats your heart to keep you going. 

My inner child just so happens to be an overly energetic, curious brat who keeps drawing on the walls.

Who's yours?
