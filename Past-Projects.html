PAST PROJECTS

breakfast

"Breakfast" was a way to get inattentive students more involved in the classes. By meeting with certain students before class for a cup of coffee and a chat, the student had something to talk about during class. This activity brough class involvement up very quickly.

art

"Art" was one of my more successful projects with student involvement. I chose certain talented students and helped them pursue their art to a more international audience. Students involved in this project quickly improved their English knowledge due to constant communications and correspondences.

travel

"Travel" was by far one of the most exceptional projects I had ever put together. Students who wish to drastically accelerate their learning had the choice to block all influence of their social influence by taking some time in an other country. I would be their silent guide. This forced to students to pick up the common phrases of the language in just a matter of days.
